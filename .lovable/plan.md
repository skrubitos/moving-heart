

# Kinesis Transport — Landing Page

## Overview
A professional, high-converting black & white landing page for a logistics company specializing in furniture transport and relocation.

## Sections

### 1. Hero Section
- Bold headline: "Safe Moves. Reliable Logistics. Your Partner in Motion."
- Subtext for B2B and residential segments
- Two CTA buttons: "Get a Quote" and "Call Now"
- Scroll-reveal animations with Framer Motion

### 2. Services Section
- Three animated cards with Lucide icons:
  - **Furniture Delivery** — store-to-home handling
  - **Residential Relocation** — apartment/house moves
  - **B2B Logistics** — dedicated business solutions
- Hover effects and staggered fade-in animations

### 3. Trust & Safety Section
- Emphasis on cargo insurance, modern equipment, and reliability
- Visual trust indicators (icons + stats)
- Clean layout with gray accents

### 4. Contact/Conversion Section (3-Way CTA)
- **Call Now** — tel: link button
- **WhatsApp Us** — quick engagement button
- **Contact Form** — Name, Phone, Service Description, Send Request
- No pricing shown; goal is lead generation

### 5. Footer
- Company info, quick links, contact details

## Design
- **Palette**: Black, white, grays — minimalist and premium
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif (Inter or system fonts)
- **Animations**: Framer Motion scroll-reveal, hover transitions, staggered card animations
- **Layout**: Mobile-first responsive design
- **Images**: Grayscale placeholder imagery

## Technical
- Install `framer-motion`
- Modular component structure
- Mobile-first Tailwind CSS
